Neptune


Neptune is the eighth and farthest known planet from the Sun in our solar system. It is classified as an ice giant, along with Uranus, due to its substantial composition of ice and gas.Neptune is the fourth-largest planet by diameter and the third largest by mass in the solar system.Its atmosphere is predominantly composed of hydrogen (about 80%) and helium (about 19%), with trace amounts of other gases like methane, ammonia, and water vapor.Neptune is known for its striking blue color, which is the result of the absorption and scattering of sunlight by methane in its atmosphere.
