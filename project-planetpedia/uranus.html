Uranus


Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun in our solar system. It is classified as an ice giant, similar to Neptune, because it has a relatively small rocky core surrounded by a thick layer of icy and gaseous materials. Uranus is known for its distinct bluish-green color, which is the result of the absorption and scattering of sunlight by methane in its atmosphere. It is named after the ancient Greek god of the sky, Uranus.
