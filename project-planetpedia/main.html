Earth


Earth is the third planet from the Sun in our solar system and is the only known celestial body to support life. It is often referred to as the 'Blue Planet' due to its abundant water, which covers about 71% of its surface.Life on Earth is concentrated in a relatively thin layer at and near the surface, where conditions are suitable for living organisms. Earth has one natural satellite, the Moon, which has a significant influence on Earth's tides and has been a subject of scientific exploration and cultural significance throughout human history
